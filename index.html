Open Cell

The best places for open cell spray foam are attics, rim joists, crawl spaces, or open wall cavities because it is a very malleable and lightweight insulation material.

Closed Cell

As the name implies, closed cell foam is made up of entirely closed cells. Because the cells are pushed together, air and moisture cannot enter the foam. As a result, closed cell foam is significantly more stable and rigid than open cell foam.
